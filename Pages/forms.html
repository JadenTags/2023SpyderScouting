Pre-Scouting
Occurs before the competition starts. Pre-Scouting gets all of a robot's information. This will occur at the very beginning of the competition.

Pit-Scouting
Occurs before our upcoming matches. Pit scouting gets any changes in data and also asks for certain data that the teams get a feel of throughout the competition.

Match Scouting
Occurs whenever. VODs of games will be reviewed and the stats of each robot's performance during the match will be recorded.
